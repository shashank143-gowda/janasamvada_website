About JanSamvaad

Empowering rural communities through digital innovation

JanSamvaad (People's Voice) is a comprehensive platform designed to empower rural communities by providing easy access to essential services, government schemes, and tools to report local issues.

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide and ensure that every citizen, regardless of their location or technical expertise, can benefit from digital governance and services.
