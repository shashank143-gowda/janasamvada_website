Benefits of Tree Planting

Clean Air

Trees absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen, improving air quality and reducing pollution.

Water Conservation

Trees help in water conservation by preventing soil erosion and increasing groundwater recharge.

Climate Regulation

Trees help in regulating local climate by providing shade and reducing the urban heat island effect.

Biodiversity

Trees provide habitat for various species of birds, insects, and animals, promoting biodiversity.

Food Security

Fruit-bearing trees provide nutritious food and can contribute to food security in rural areas.

Economic Benefits

Trees can provide economic benefits through fruits, timber, and other products, supporting livelihoods.
